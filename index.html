    What is "The Blockchain"?

    While many still argue whether Bitcoin and other currencies will replace Fiat currencies, the significance annd revolutionary nature of blockchain technology is never contended. In the simplest sense, the blockchain is a decentralized global ledger that is both public yet anonymous. Based on mathematic and cryptography, this model is able to overcome many of the obstacles of previus currencies while removing the middle-man from the equation (banks, goverments, credit card companies, etc.). With your currency on the blockchain, your wealth is unhackable, easily transferable, and most importantLy, under your sole control and ownership.

    3

    Currency is Only One Use

    While most people think of money when they think of Bitcoin and blockchain technology, the possible uses are endless. Blockchain technology can be applied to record keeping, voting, shipment of goods, advertisement, quality inspection, data collection, data storage, artificial intellegence, shared computing power, and social netowkring - just to name a few examples. With several large companies such as IBM, Microsoft, Google, and Tesla either adopting or support blockchain tech, we are set to see a huge paradigm shift in the next few years.
